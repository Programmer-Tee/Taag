About us

Taag Logistixs, is a company that specializes in logistics using state of the art technology as well as well trained and customer friendly staff. In today's fast moving world, we ensure our clients reliability, speed, accuracy, security and value. Based in Lagos, Nigeria, our logistics experts work directly with our clients to optimize their supply chains by developing and implementing customized transportation solutions that best fit their needs.

At Taag Logistixs our services allows businesses move their goods and services in an effective, scalable and cost effective manner.

We provide courier services such as last mile delivery for SME’S and E- Commerce businesses at a competitive price.

Our pricing model is competitive and flexible. We pride ourselves in offering a value based pricing andour rates are among the keenest in the industry.
